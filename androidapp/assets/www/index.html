FAQ

1. What does this app do?

Reports a spam call/SMS,also known as Unsolicited Commercial Communication(UCC), to the concerned authority.

2. How it is different from other spam blockers apps?

Other apps work locally.Which means they hide UCC from your phone. This app, however, offers something more permament.Reported number will never be able to send a UCC again.

3. How does it do that?

It extracts these info - time,date and number. Formats them into a string and sends it to 1909(specified by TRAI)

4. Can i do this without this app?

Sure But do keep a notebook handy to note down all details and then compose them into a formidable looking string.:)

5. What happens when I report to a UCC.

First of all it gets deleted from your phone(you can turn off/on this feature under "Score" screen).After that, it depends on your operator. Here is what happens with Vodafone(Delhi).I get a confirmation from 1909 about my report. I also get a complaint number.Within next two days a customer care representative calls me and tells me that they have issued a warning to the sender to never send a UCC to my number. There are fines for a second incidence etc.

6. Does it cost me money?

No. 1909 is toll-free.

7. Why do i keep getting errors about wrong format?

Because each network operator implements there own format, even though TRAI has specified one universal format.

8. What are other common errors I can get?

"Default not configured" "Generic failure error" "Service not available" I can't do any thing about it as they are errors on provider's side. Please contact your customer care.

9. Why don't I see all my calls/sms?

This is a feature. TRAI directs that UCC should be reported within 3 days of receiving. So I hide SMS/calls older than three days.

10. Cool. I like your app. Is there something you expect in return?

Yes,in fact. I hate spam as much as you do.That's why I created this app(notice, no ads).But for it to be effective, more people should start using it. So I urge you to spread the word.Use Facebook to tell your friends.
